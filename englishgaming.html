Study Corner — Present Perfect
  • Forma: have/has + past participle (have eaten, has seen).
  • Usos: experiência (I've been to Paris), duração (I have lived here for 3 years), ações recentes com resultado (She has finished her work).
  • Palavras comuns: ever, never, already, yet, for, since.
